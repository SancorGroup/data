Strategic Roadmap: L&W Supply Direct Acquisition

This phased plan outlines the specific actions and timelines for L&W Supply to execute the direct acquisition and immediate development, ensuring a seamless transition and maximum value capture.

Phase 1: Immediate Action (0-3 Months)

  • Secure the land with a Purchase and Sale Agreement.
  • Engage project team (Architect, Engineer, General Contractor, Environmental Consultant).
  • Initiate talks with lenders to lock in 6% fixed-rate financing at a 70% loan-to-cost.

Phase 2: Entitlement & Permitting (3-9 Months)

  • Submit final plans for zoning and site plan review.
  • Obtain all necessary building permits.
  • Proactively engage with local officials to streamline the process.
  • Coordinate with utility providers for tie-ins.

Phase 3: Construction & Occupancy (9-20 Months)

  • Break ground and begin construction under a GMP contract.
  • Implement a project management system for progress tracking.
  • Complete final inspections for the certificate of occupancy.
  • Begin interior build-out and occupy the new facility.
